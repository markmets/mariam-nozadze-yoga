About

Hello, I'm
Mariam

My path to yoga began as a personal quest for stillness in a loud world — and it changed everything.

I'm a certified 500-hour RYT yoga instructor based in Georgia, specialising in Hatha, Vinyasa, and Restorative yoga. I believe every practice is deeply personal, and I design sessions that meet you exactly where you are — whether you're taking your first breath on the mat or deepening an established practice.

  • 500-hr RYT Certified — Yoga Alliance
  • Yin Yoga & Restorative Specialisation
  • Pranayama & Meditation Teacher

Hatha Yoga
